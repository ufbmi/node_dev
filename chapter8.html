Personal Management Plan

The best insulin regimen is the
BASAL - BOLUS regimen

BASAL insulin is the Long-acting insulin

Lantus or Levemir

BOLUS insulin is the Short-acting insulin

Humalog, Novolog, and Apidra

How often is insulin given?

  • BASAL insulin is given once or twice a day

  • BOLUS insulin is given any time food is given or glucose level is high (usually 3-4 times a day)

    • How to calculate BOLUS dose

  • Carb ratio: covers the amout of carbs that are going to be eaten
    Grams of carbs / Your Carb Ratio = units of insulin

  • Correction factor: covers the blodd glucose if its high before the meal
    (Blood glucose - Target) / Sensitivity = units of insulin
